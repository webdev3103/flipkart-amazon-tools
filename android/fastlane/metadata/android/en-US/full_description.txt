Sacred Sutra Tools is a comprehensive inventory and order management application designed for Sacred Sutra business operations.

Features:
• Real-time inventory tracking
• Order management and analytics
• Barcode scanning for quick product lookup
• Returns processing and analytics
• Category and product management
• Transaction analytics and reporting
• Firebase-powered cloud sync
• Offline support with PWA capabilities

Perfect for managing your Sacred Sutra inventory, processing orders, and analyzing business metrics on the go.
